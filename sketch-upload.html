Tips for a good sketch

Label every wall dimension clearly — e.g. 5000mm or 5.0m
Note ceiling height on the plan — write h=2700 anywhere
Mark windows and doors — a gap in the wall with a W or D label is fine
Take the photo straight on, in good light — avoid shadows across the page
Don't worry about perfect draughtsmanship — rough is fine as long as dimensions are legible
